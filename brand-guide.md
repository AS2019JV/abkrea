# Abkrea Ingeniería - Brand & Style Guide
**Document Version:** 1.0.0  
**Target Aesthetic:** Tech-Luxury & Industrial Precision  
**Core Target Audience:** Engineers, Technical Professionals, and Corporate Clients (Ecuador)

---

## 1. Brand Identity & Strategic Positioning

### 1.1 Core Value Proposition
Abkrea is an elite engineering and technical training center based in Ambato, Ecuador. The brand embodies precision, corporate reliability, and institutional excellence. 

### 1.2 The Ultimate Strategic Asset (Core Advantage)
The absolute cornerstone of Abkrea’s brand authority is its official qualification code: **SETEC-CAL-2018-092** (regulated under MDT / Senescyt in Ecuador). This certification separates Abkrea from amateur academies and must be displayed prominently as a high-trust verification badge across all brand materials and web layouts.

### 1.3 Voice and Tone Guidance
* **Authoritative & Professional:** Speak as an industry leader in engineering. Never use casual slang or generic placeholder text.
* **Precise & Technical:** Use correct industry nomenclature when describing modules (e.g., *SolidWorks Flow Simulation, Structural Members, Rhinoceros Advanced Modeling*).
* **Corporate & Secure:** Avoid looking like an indie software project. Every interaction must project institutional backing and strict data security.

---

## 2. Visual Identity System

### 2.1 Logo Specifications
* **Deprecated Asset:** The low-resolution `100x100-01-e1595562931406.jpg` image is strictly banned. It creates an amateur impression on high-density (Retina/OLED) screens.
* **Current Requirement:** The logo must be rendered as a scalable, lossless vector file (`.svg`). 
* **Clearance & Padding:** Maintain a minimum clear space equal to 20% of the logo's width around all sides to ensure visual breathing room.

### 2.2 Color Palette (Industrial Precision Tech-Luxury)
To move away from generic, unconfigured WordPress theme blues, Abkrea adopts a high-contrast, premium engineering color scheme:

| Color Name | Hex Code | Purpose / Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Industrial Navy** | `#0F172A` | Primary brand color, headers, structural layout backgrounds |
| **Precision Blue** | `#2563EB` | Interactive highlights, call-to-actions (CTAs), focus states |
| **Titanium Silver** | `#E2E8F0` | Subtle borders, light section backgrounds, card containers |
| **Deep Charcoal** | `#1E293B` | Body typography for maximum readability and crisp contrast |
| **Safety Amber** | `#F59E0B` | Exclusively for high-value highlights (e.g., SETEC Certification Badges) |

### 2.3 Typography Scale
The typography system relies on clean, highly readable, geometric sans-serif typefaces designed for technical layouts (e.g., **Inter** or **SF Pro**).

* **H1 (Page Titles):** 24pt / Bold / Industrial Navy / Margin Bottom: 16px
* **H2 (Section Headers):** 18pt / Semi-Bold / Industrial Navy / Margin Bottom: 12px
* **H3 (Subsections & Cards):** 14pt / Medium / Deep Charcoal / Margin Bottom: 8px
* **Body Text:** 10.5pt / Regular / Deep Charcoal / Line Height: 1.5
* **Captions/Badges:** 9pt / Mono or Bold / Uppercase / Safety Amber or White

## 3. Web & UI/UX Content Guardrails

### 3.1 Trust Infrastructure Rules
* **No External Theme Leaks:** Under no circumstances should buttons like "Read More" or "View Design" redirect to external theme providers (e.g., ThemeGrill). All CTAs must route internally or link to official automated pipelines.
* **No Institutional Email Dilution:** The personal Gmail address (`jp.muquinche@gmail.com`) is completely prohibited on public channels. All contact information must use the official corporate domain (`@abkrea.com`).
* **Course Card Grid Layout Rules:** Text flows inside product/course cards must be strictly bounded. Text strings must be truncated or standardized to prevent layout breakage (e.g., the SolidWorks text overflow error must be resolved with absolute vertical alignment symmetry across adjacent cards like Electricity and Rhinoceros).
* **Zero-Tolerance for Orphan Template Content:** All default WordPress/template sample posts (e.g., *"Mac Book Air and a cup of Coffee"*, *"Hello World!"*) must be completely scrubbed from databases and production layouts.

## 1. Brand Identity & Strategic Positioning

### 1.1 Core Value Proposition
Abkrea is an elite engineering and advanced technical training institution based in Ambato, Ecuador. Unlike generic online bootcamps, Abkrea delivers rigorous, industry-compliant training tailored to modern engineering standards (CAD/CAM/CAE, electrical grids, and parametric design). The brand voice balances advanced technical mastery with premium corporate execution.

### 1.2 The Ultimate Strategic Asset (SETEC Competitive Advantage)
The absolute cornerstone of Abkrea’s brand authority is its official qualification code: **SETEC-CAL-2018-092** (regulated under the Ministry of Labor - MDT and Senescyt framework in Ecuador). 

In the Ecuadorian industrial B2B market, this asset provides an unassailable competitive advantage:
* **Tax Deductibility (Deducibilidad de Impuestos):** Corporate clients can deduct employee training investments from their annual corporate income tax calculations when training with an officially certified institution.
* **Public Tender Compliance:** State-owned enterprises and large industrial firms require formal SETEC-certified hours for employee validation and public procurement compliance.
* **Institutional Credibility:** It differentiates Abkrea from non-certified, casual courses by guaranteeing curriculum vetting and official physical/digital certificate validity.

**Brand Guardrail:** This certification must never be buried in a footer. It must be presented as a "Trust Badge" in high-visibility zones (Header, Main Value Hero Section, Course Product Cards, and the checkout or quote request workflows).

### 1.3 Voice and Tone Guidance
* **Authoritative & Industrial:** Speak as an industry peer to engineers and decision-makers. Use clear, unembellished terms.
* **Precise & Scientific:** Reference specific software versions, industry standards, and structural modeling modules (e.g., *SolidWorks FEA Simulation, Rhinoceros Grasshopper parametric frameworks, NEMA electrical standards*).
* **Premium Tech-Luxury:** Maintain a clean, high-end presentation reminiscent of elite technological companies (e.g., Apple Pro, Stripe, Autodesk Enterprise). Avoid flashy, discount-driven digital marketing language.

## 2. Visual Identity System

### 2.1 Logo Specifications & Geometry
* **Modern Lossless Vectorization:** The deprecated asset (`100x100-01-e1595562931406.jpg`) is completely banned. The logo must be rendered purely as an optimized `.svg` asset.
* **Layout Breathing Room:** Maintain an explicit bounding container padding of `1.5x` the logo's width to ensure it stands out clearly within navigation panels without crowding technical text.

### 2.2 Color Palette (Industrial Precision Tech-Luxury)
Abkrea transitions away from generic, unconfigured template colors into an elite, highly structured chromatic system:

| Color Name | Hex Code | System Deployment | Architectural Intention |
